Nikhat Thanedar is a doctor specializing in radiology is called a radiologist. Radiologists are medical professionals who use imaging techniques such as X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, ultrasounds, and nuclear medicine to diagnose and sometimes treat diseases. They interpret medical images to assist other physicians in determining the best course of care. Radiologists may also specialize further in areas like interventional radiology, where they perform minimally invasive procedures guided by imaging. They require extensive training, including medical school, a residency in radiology, and often additional fellowship training.
